## How benchmarks are used

- Each post has a `category` in frontmatter
- SEO optimization compares the target post against the best performer in its category
- New metadata should aim to beat the category benchmark, not copy another category's numbers

**Winning formula notes:**

- Benefit-first description (what reader learns, not "comprehensive guide")
- Plain-English title with specific scope ("Token Economics", not "Everything About Tokens")
- Tables and verifiable numbers increase AI-tool citations
- FAQ on long posts
- 2–5 internal links to pillar/cluster posts

## Anti-patterns (all categories)

- "Complete", "Comprehensive", "Ultimate", "Definitive" in title
- Description that repeats the title verbatim
- Empty CTAs: "Ready to get started?", "Let's go!", "Dive in!"
- Fake statistics without attribution
- Title over 65 characters without good reason
