## Voice rules distilled

- **I** for personal experiments; **we** for team/project experience. Pick one per post and stay consistent.
- Name real products, models, and dates. Cite official docs when claiming behavior.
- Admit uncertainty ("as of May 2026", "likely", "no official confirmation").
- Anti-hype: separate marketing from what you tested.
- Dry humor OK. Meme-speak and LinkedIn-bro energy are not.
- Editorial tone, not a product page.

Voice and anti-AI rules work together: human voice comes from specifics and opinions, not from breaking detector limits.
